Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

1. Data Collection

Baby Tracker does not collect personal data such as name, email address, phone number, or precise location. All baby tracking records are stored locally on the user’s device.

2. Advertising

The app may display advertisements provided by third-party ad networks. These providers may collect limited device information (such as advertising identifiers, IP address, and basic device signals) to serve ads, prevent fraud, and measure performance.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We do not share locally stored user records with third parties. Advertising partners may process limited device information as described above.

4. Children’s Privacy

Baby Tracker is intended for parents and caregivers. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

5. Security

Since all data is stored locally on the device, device-level security measures (such as passcode or biometric protection) are important for data protection.
